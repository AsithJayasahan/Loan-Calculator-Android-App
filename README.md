# Loan-Calculator-Android-App

<h2>Overview</h2>
<p></p>Loan Calculator is an Android application designed to help users calculate and manage their loan repayments. Users can input the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term to receive detailed payment schedules and overall payment summaries.</p>

<h2>Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Loan Amount: Specify the total amount you wish to borrow.</li>
  <li>Interest Rate: Input the annual interest rate for the loan.</li>
  <li>Loan Term: Define the duration of the loan in years.</li>
</ul>


<h2>Usage</h2>
<ol>
  <li>Open the app on your Android device.</li>
  <li>Enter loan details including the loan amount, interest rate, and term.</li>
  <li>Click "Calculate" to see the monthly payment, total payments, and a breakdown of each payment.</li>
  
</ol>
